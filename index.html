About Me

I am a Society of Science Fellow at University of Notre Dame, and am working with Andrew Putman and Nick Salter.

I completed my PhD jointly at Universidad Autónoma de Madrid and Instituto de Ciencias Matemáticas, advised by Javier Aramayona.

My research concerns the interactions between group theory, geometry and topology. The main objects I investigate are mapping class groups of Riemann surfaces and moduli spaces. I am also interested in the structure of algebro-geometric groups such as braid groups, surface groups, free groups, and the like.
